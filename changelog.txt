2026-06-18 (addendum) — Stop Telegram spam from transient network errors
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
main.py on_error handler
  - Was forwarding EVERY error to the chat, including PTB's routine long-poll
    NetworkError/TimedOut (e.g. httpx.ReadError) that PTB auto-retries -> chat
    spam. Now: transient network/httpx/httpcore errors are logged at WARNING and
    NOT sent to chat. Genuine errors are still surfaced, but throttled (same
    message not re-sent within 60s) so a repeating failure can't flood the chat.

2026-07-08 (addendum) — Reconciliation false positive on post-close approvals
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
src/trading_system/safety/reconciliation.py
  - FIXED: reconciliation flagged "LOCAL open: X — not found in Alpaca" for every
    trade approved after the 16:00 close. Root cause: the scanner fires at 16:05
    ET (after close), so an approved market/DAY bracket order is ACCEPTED by
    Alpaca but queued for the next open — no position exists yet at the 16:30
    reconciliation. Reconciliation only inspected positions, not orders, so it
    couldn't tell "pending fill" from "truly gone".
  - Now order-aware (same "never assume gone" rule the position monitor uses):
    fetches open orders; a local open trade that is UNFILLED (fill_price IS NULL)
    and has a matching accepted/pending Alpaca order is reported as "⏳ pending
    fill", not a mismatch. A filled-but-missing position, or an unfilled trade
    with NO Alpaca order, is still flagged as real drift.

tests/unit/test_reconciliation.py
  - FakeClient gained get_orders; _open_trade takes fill_price.
  - New: pending-order → not a mismatch; no-order → mismatch; filled-but-missing
    → mismatch. 60 -> 63 tests.
